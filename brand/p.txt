Nexus Systems — Privacy Policy
Last updated: July 9, 2026


1. OVERVIEW

This Privacy Policy explains what information CH Services ("we," "us,"
"our") collects through the Nexus Systems Discord bot and web dashboard
(together, the "Service"), how we use it, and the choices available to
you. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of
information as described here.


2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

2.1 Information from Discord. When Nexus Systems is added to a server,
or when you log into the web dashboard via Discord OAuth2, we may
receive:
  • Discord user IDs, usernames, and avatars
  • Discord server (guild) IDs and names
  • Role and channel IDs relevant to features you configure
  • The list of servers you have access to, solely to determine which
    servers you are authorized to manage in the dashboard

2.2 Content Processed for Moderation and Features. To provide
moderation and community features, the Service processes:
  • Message content, for AutoMod filtering (e.g. banned words, invite
    links, spam detection) and custom command matching
  • Moderation records you or your staff create: warnings, mod notes,
    ban/kick/timeout logs, and the reasons provided for them
  • Feature-specific data you configure or generate: tickets,
    suggestions, giveaway entries, leveling/XP data, starboard posts,
    reaction-role mappings, and custom embeds

2.3 Dashboard Sessions. The web dashboard uses session cookies to keep
you logged in. We do not use third-party advertising or tracking
cookies.

2.4 We Do Not Collect. We do not collect your Discord password (OAuth2
never shares it with us), payment information, or data from Discord
servers the bot has not been added to.


3. HOW WE USE INFORMATION

We use the information described above to:
  • Operate the Service's moderation, security, and engagement features
  • Display server configuration and activity in the web dashboard
  • Enforce permissions (including Teams and role-based access you
    configure) so only authorized users can manage a given server
  • Maintain logs (mod actions, message edits/deletes, join/leave
    events) that your server's staff has chosen to enable
  • Diagnose and fix technical issues

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for
marketing purposes.


4. DATA STORAGE

Data is stored in a database operated by CH Services, used exclusively
to run the Service. Data for each Discord server is logically
separated by server (guild) ID. We apply reasonable technical measures
(such as restricted database access and encrypted transport) to
protect stored data, but no method of storage or transmission is
completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.


5. DATA RETENTION

We retain data for as long as Nexus Systems remains active in a
server, or as needed to provide the Service. If Nexus Systems is
removed from a server, associated configuration and records may be
retained for a reasonable period afterward (in case the bot is
re-added) and may be deleted thereafter. You may request deletion of
your server's data by contacting us, as described in Section 8.


6. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

The Service operates on top of Discord's platform and API. Discord's
own collection and use of your information is governed by Discord's
Privacy Policy, which we do not control. Where the Service is deployed
on cloud infrastructure (e.g., a hosting provider), that provider may
process data on our behalf solely to operate the Service.


7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

The Service is intended for use in accordance with Discord's own
minimum age requirements. We do not knowingly collect information from
individuals below the minimum age required to use Discord.


8. YOUR CHOICES AND RIGHTS

  • Server staff can disable specific features (e.g., logging,
    leveling, AutoMod content scanning) at any time via the dashboard,
    which stops new data collection for that feature going forward.
  • You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of data
    associated with your Discord server or user ID by contacting the
    CH Services team through the official Nexus Systems Discord
    server.
  • Removing Nexus Systems from your server stops all further data
    collection from that server.


9. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes
will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued
use of the Service after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of
the revised policy.


10. CONTACT

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be directed to the CH Services
team through the official Nexus Systems Discord server.
